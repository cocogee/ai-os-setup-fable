# 02_business — What your business is

**Type:** Static context (changes monthly, not daily).

**Belongs here:** Overview, customers, offers, marketing, operations — the facts Claude needs to give business advice that fits YOUR business.

**Does not belong here:** Live numbers (revenue, email, calendar — those are connections, 05_connections), or project work (03_projects).

**Before modifying:** Check the file's "Last updated" date. If updating numbers, note the date and the source so stale data is detectable later.
